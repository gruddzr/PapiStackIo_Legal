# Privacy Policy - Annur Period Tracker

**Effective Date: April 1, 2026**

Assalamu’alaikum! Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. The **Annur Period Tracker** ("the App") is designed specifically for the Muslimah community to track their menstrual cycles (Haydh) and purity (Tuhr) in accordance with Islamic Fiqh. We take a "Privacy-First" approach to all data handling.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect
We do **NOT** collect, store, or sell any of your personal health data to our servers. All sensitive cycle information, including:
- Dates of menstruation and purity.
- Fiqh preferences (Madhhab).
- Physical symptoms (flow intensity, colors).
- Birthdate or name.

**Is stored locally on your device only.** This means we have no access to it, and if you delete the App, all your cycle data is permanently removed unless you have manually exported it.

## 2. Third-Party Services
To support the development of this free application, we utilize the following third-party services which may collect limited, non-identifiable data:

### A. Google AdMob (Advertising)
Since our target audience includes users aged 9 and older, we strictly comply with Google's Families Policy and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
- **Target Audience Compliance**: We inform AdMob that the App is directed at a "mixed audience" (including children).
- **No Personalized Ads**: We disable "personalized" (interest-based) advertising for all users.
- **Content Rating**: We restrict all advertisements to those rated **"G" (General Audience)** or **"PG"**.
- [Link to Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### B. RevenueCat (Store Purchases)
If you choose to purchase **Annur Premium**, RevenueCat manages your receipt and subscription status. 
- They do not have access to your health logs or personal cycle data.
- [Link to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

## 3. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K Compliance)
We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. 
- We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
- We treat the entire App as child-directed for the purposes of advertising and tracking, ensuring strict data protections for our younger users.

## 4. Permissions
The App may request the following permissions on your device:
- **Internet**: To load advertisements and verify Premium status.
- **Location**: To calculate accurate prayer times (Salah) and the Hijri date. This location data is processed locally and not stored on our servers.

## 5. Your Choices
You have full control over your data.
- **Data Export**: You can export your data at any time in JSON or PDF format from the Settings.
- **Data Deletion**: You can "Wipe All Data" from the Settings menu to permanently delete all local logs.
